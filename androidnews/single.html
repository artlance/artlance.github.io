5 Best Android Apps to Plan Menus and Organize your Meals

7 October, 2020 — James Brandon
Android Apps Cooking

Having a busy schedule will more likely than not interfere with your meal schedule. You may end up not knowing what to cook for yourself or your family, and even end up buying unhealthy fast foods.

With the help of menu planner apps, you can plan ahead for what you and your family will eat throughout the week or month so that you will not have to think of what to prepare or eat every time. This piece highlights 5 of the best Android apps that you can use to plan menus and organize your meals.

  1. Eat This Much – Meal Planner

    With this automatic meal planner, you will always know what to eat. Using the app, you can set your diet goals, the foods you like, the budget you are working with, and what your schedule looks like. The app will then generate a complete meal plan that meets your requirements. You can also use the app to set nutrition targets for weight loss, maintenance, or muscle/bodybuilding. The app has a variety of recipes that you can use or customize to your liking.

    Get it on Google play

  2. Plan Meals – MealPlanner

    As the name suggests, the app is an easy-to-use meal planner that will help you to plan your meals from week to week. The app has a friendly user interface that allows any user to interact with it. You can also use the app to create your own recipes that you can share with friends and other family members.

    Get it on Google play

  3. Plan to Eat : Meal Planner & Shopping List Maker

    With this app, you can change your eating habits, remain healthier, and save a lot of money related to food. The app contains a cookbook that contains recipes for you to use. You can also import recipes from webpages into the app. The app allows you to share your meal plans and recipes with your friends via social media platforms. The app also allows you to shop with a purpose as it automatically adds the ingredients to be used in specific meals to your shopping list. You can also manually add shopping list items.

    Get it on Google play

  4. Recipe Calendar – Meal Planner

    Recipe Calendar is a time saver when it comes to cooking. The app allows you to choose a meal plan, provides a grocery list for every meal you will want to prepare, and give you step-by-step directions on how to prepare each meal. The app provides a variety of healthy diets including low-carb, vegetarian, low-calorie, and paleo menus. You can create meal schedules that include a certain number of people and update your schedule as often as possible.

    Get it on Google play

  5. Prepear – Meal Planner, Grocery List, & Recipes

    The Prepear app allows you to plan your meals, create and manage your grocery lists, and explore recipes. You will collect recipes from anywhere and import them into the app as cookbooks. You can also share your cookbooks with friends. With the meal planning feature of the app, you can organize your entire meal process and add recipes that you will use during the week. The app also features automatic grocery lists that will help you sort ingredients into categories for efficient shopping.

    Get it on Google play
