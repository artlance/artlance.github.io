About the station

Greek based online Hip-Hop & RNB music radio station streaming in a variety of formats. Thanks to exclusive partnerships with successful hip-hop music labels we are able to play the newest and hottest tracks and mixes 24/7. Residents and other well known and international famous Djs have their own radio shows frequently.
